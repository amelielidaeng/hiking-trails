Family friendly

Our easy trails is good for you and your small children.The terrain is mostly flat and there is a lot of shallow lakes for picnic and swimming

Our medium trails are longer with a more hilly terrain. This is in general good trails for mounting biking. The medium trails are not recommended for the smallest children.

You will have fun with you larger kids with geocaching by locate fixed installed things in the nature, in our geocache areas. More information in our geocache site and trail map site.
