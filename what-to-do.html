What to do

In our trail parks there is a lot of activities. In this nature we also have culture. So if you like doing fun things in the nature, than you should check out our activities in this page.

In summer time we have stuff every day i july, and in the weekends between may and september. Here you can read more about our staff and what we do.

Landmarks

There is a lot of landmarks to see. This old cannon is one of them. If you go far enough, you will see the most spectacular things.

Wild life

The wild animals and plats is the citizens of the nature and they are in charge here. It means that we are not aloud to disturb the animals, break branches or pick flowers.

Family friendly

We make sure that you get the right information when visiting our parks with your kids. The children comes first, so check out the information for each trails.

Geocache

We have areas for both geocache competitions and non competition. We provide different styles of geocache collecting based on if there is competitions or not.
