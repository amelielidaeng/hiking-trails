Geocache

We arranges geocache competitions. We have both general and national competitions.

National competitions

We arrange national competitions for Geocaching. It is similar to orientation, but the target have to be located by its coordinate and is often hidden. The contestants of the national competition don't collect a psysical object. Instead They have to solve maths, riddles and other fact questions. No internet device is allowed.The winner is the one with most score in both right answers and total time when finished.

General competitions

Our general competitions is different from the national. The target have have to be collected, so here you will have a bag with you. The winner is the one who collected the most objects.

Non competitions

Here you train your skills without competing. Its a good activity for family. The objects are a fixed installation, so you only take a picture and show it to the staff when finished.
