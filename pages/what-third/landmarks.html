Land marks

here is a collection of landmarks that you can see when visiting our trails. Especially the old castle is a popular visit.

The lighthouse

The old red and white lighthouse is famous and have a lot of history. In the history people were thrown down from the top of the lighthouse when sleeping on there guaring post.

The old oak

This oak. around thousand years old and 6 meters around 1,5 meters up from the ground. There is around two hundred species living on the oak, mostly lichens. The oak is protected by this reason and for its history.

The old castle

The old castle was build for the kings of the middle ages. Today it is partly a ruin, which mean safety restrictions when visiting. By the foot of the castle there is a lot of information about its history.

The ghost house

This old house is based on a open old farm area, which is now only grass. The last owner were killed with his family in the house hundred years ago. The story tells that the family is still in there and you can sometimes here them talking to each other.

The Edgy Rock

You can see this rock from very far away. It does'nt look like the rest of the rock in the area beacuse it have trees alost up to the top of the rock.

The Man Stone

This stone sculpture has been there for a very long time. Its four meters high and two and a half meters width, and it's based on top of a larger cliff. Nobody knows who make this sculpture. This is the big mystery in all trails.
