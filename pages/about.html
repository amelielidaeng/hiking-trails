About Swedish Hiking Trails

We are a group of outdoors people and nature lovers who decided to get together and gather our favorite trails and landmarks in one place. Over the years we grew tired of searching multiple websites and social media outlets to find new spots to visit, so we decided to create a space where we can collect it all.

I you have any questions, tips or general praise, please send us a message right here!
